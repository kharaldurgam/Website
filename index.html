Where Himalayan Flavor Meets Helsinki

Handmade dumplings crafted daily in Helsinki, inspired by Nepali kitchen traditions and cooked with rich, comforting flavor.

What is Mo:Mo?

Momo is a traditional Nepali dumpling filled with delicious ingredients such as chicken, vegetables or mutton. Steamed or fried, mo:mo are served with flavorful sauces and spices that bring the authentic taste of the Himalayas to every bite.

Our Popular Dishes

Fried Mo:mo

Crispy on the outside and tender on the inside, these fried dumplings are filled with a savory blend of meat and spices, making them a favorite among our customers.

Vegetable Mo:mo

A delightful vegetarian option featuring a mix of fresh vegetables and aromatic herbs, steamed to perfection.

Steam Mo:mo

Soft and juicy, these steamed dumplings are filled with a flavorful combination of meat and spices, offering a comforting and satisfying dining experience.
